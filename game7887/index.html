Loan Eligibility Criteria

While criteria vary by bank and loan type, here are general requirements:

CIBIL Score Importance 750+ ideal
Income Stability 2+ years preferred
  • Minimum age: 21 years, Maximum age: 60-65 years at loan maturity (up to 70 for some pensioners)
  • Minimum income: ₹15,000-₹25,000 per month depending on city and employment type (lower for government employees)
  • CIBIL score of 750+ preferred for best interest rates (some banks accept 650+)
  • Stable employment history (minimum 2-3 years for salaried individuals)
  • Business vintage of 3+ years for self-employed applicants (1+ year for some new businesses)
  • Proper documentation including ID proof, address proof, income proof, and property documents
  • Debt-to-income ratio below 50-60% (including the new loan EMI)
  • Property valuation and legal checks for secured loans
  • Educational qualifications considered for education loans and professional loans

Frequently Asked Questions

Loan amounts vary by type: Home loans up to ₹10 crore (depends on property value), personal loans typically ₹10-40 lakhs based on income, business loans up to ₹2 crore or more for established businesses. Lenders generally offer 10-15 times your monthly income for unsecured loans.

Processing times vary: Personal loans can be approved in 1 hour to 3 days, home loans take 2-7 days for approval (plus property evaluation time), business loans typically 3-10 days depending on documentation. Pre-approved offers are instant.

Most loans allow prepayment after 6-12 months. For floating rate loans, RBI guidelines prohibit prepayment charges after 3 years. Fixed rate loans may have 2-5% prepayment charges. Some banks offer free partial prepayments (usually limited to 25% of outstanding per year).
