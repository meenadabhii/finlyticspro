Required Documents

For Salaried Individuals

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID)
  • Address Proof (Utility bill, Rental agreement, Aadhaar)
  • Latest 3 months salary slips
  • Form 16 or last 2 years IT returns
  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Employment certificate

For Self-Employed Professionals

  • Identity and Address Proof (same as above)
  • Last 3 years IT returns with computation of income
  • Profit & Loss statement and Balance Sheet
  • Bank statements (last 12 months)
  • Business license or registration proof
  • Property documents (for secured loans)
  • Business address proof
