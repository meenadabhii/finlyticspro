FAQs

How is my loan eligibility calculated?

Banks consider factors such as income, credit score, existing obligations, and loan tenure to calculate eligibility.

Can I prepay my loan?

Yes, most banks allow prepayment with minimal or zero charges. Always check prepayment clauses before signing.

Is insurance mandatory with loans?

Insurance isn't mandatory but is often bundled with loans for added security, especially in home and car loans.
